  • 1 tbsp rolled oats
  • 1/2 tbsp cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk (regular or any plant-based milk of choice)
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 to 1 cup fruits of choice (papaya, strawberries, bananas, etc.)
  1. Mix dry ingredients (oats, cocoa, vanilla) until no clumps remain.
  2. Add eggs and milk, mix until homogenous.
  3. Cook in non-stick pan for 5-10 mins, stirring until thick like pudding consistency. Cool briefly.
  4. Stir in honey.
  5. Top with Â½-1 cup of fruits. Time to dig in!
